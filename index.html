About Me

After living in sunny Gainesville, FL for 25 years I moved to Charlotte, NC in the dead of the winter almost 2 years ago. I am brand new to the web development world with the UNCC Coding Bootcamp being my very intense introduction. So far I am loving every bit of it and can't wait to learn more! Currently I am employed at Publix Supermarkets where I have been a cashier, event planner, and produce clerk. In my free time I love being active. I am an avid runner and just completed my first half marathon and am ready to train for my next! I also play soccer for CWSL, a women's recreational league here in Charlotte. I enjoy yoga to help me unwind. And to really relax I love reading and of course a good Netflix binge.
